5 Powerful Duas for Protection Against Harm

1: Dua for Protection from Evil Eye and Envy:

"أعوذ بكلمات الله التامة من كل شيطان وهامة، ومن كل عين لامة" (Translation: I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from every devil and every poisonous reptile, and from every envious eye.)

2: Dua for Protection from all Harm

"بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ" (Translation: In the name of Allah, with whose name nothing can cause harm on earth or in the heavens, and He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing.)

3: Dua for Protection from Evil Thoughts

"أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ" (Translation: I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He has created.)

4: Dua for Protection from Calamities

"اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْبَأسِ، وَالْجُنُونِ، وَالْجُذَامِ، وَمِنْ سَيِّئِ الْأَسْقَامِ" (Translation: O Allah, I seek refuge in You from calamity, insanity, leprosy, and evil diseases.)

5: Dua for Protection from the Evil of People

"اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ الْمُخَالَفِينَ، وَمِنْ شَرِّ أَعْدَائِكَ، وَمِنْ شَرِّ شَيَاطِينَ الْجِنِّ وَالْإِنْسِ" (Translation: O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the evil of those who oppose me, the evil of Your enemies, and the evil of the devils of the jinn and mankind.) May Allah protect and guide us all.
